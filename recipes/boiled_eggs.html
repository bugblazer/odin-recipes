Boiled Eggs

Description

What's a perfect boiled egg? The whites are firm but not rubbery and the yolks are cooked but still creamy. We'll share a fool-proof method to boil eggs on the stovetop, including how long to boil eggs so the yolks are cooked the way you like.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan or pot and cover with cold water.
  2. Put pan over high heat and bring water to a rolling boil. Remove pan from heat and cover.
  3. Drain eggs immediately and put in a bowl filled with water and ice cubes.
  4. Peel the eggs
